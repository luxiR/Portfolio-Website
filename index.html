My Favourite Book

One of Us is Lying

This murder mystery book is great, and I would recommend it to young teens
It starts off when a boy gets killed in detention, and the other people there get framed.
As the investigation starts, it seems that everyone in the room at the time had a connection to the dead person.
Was the connection so bad that they decided to end him?

The author, Karen M. McManus, has written some other great books, most of which are murder mysteries.
Some of these books include The Cousins, One of Us is Next, and Two Can Keep A Secret. She is releasing a new book, One of Us is Back, which comes out in June 2023.

Education

Dunbarton High School

This is my high school, Dunbarton. There are many clubs and councils to join here. Their mascot is the Spartan.
This school first opened in 1961, which is around 62 years ago. Their motto is Conari, Quaerer, Invenire (To Try, To Seek, To Come Upon).
The school colours are red, black, and white. In 2014, this school was voted as the Most Greenest School on Earth.
They also have a second building, mainly for the arts courses and english. This was originally a public school (Woodland PS).
